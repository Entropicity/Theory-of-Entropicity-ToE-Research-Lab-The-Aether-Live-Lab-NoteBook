# How Obidi Transformed Information Geometry Into Physical Spacetime in the Theory of Entropicity (ToE): The Insight, Concept, and Mechanism 

In the **Theory of Entropicity (ToE)**, **John Onimisi Obidi** argues that information geometry becomes physical spacetime by making a radical ontological shift: treating information geometry not as an abstract mathematical tool for classifying data, but as the actual physical substrate of reality. [1, 2] 

Under this framework, physical spacetime is a macroscopic illusion—a coarse-grained shadow cast by a deeper, high-dimensional informational manifold. Obidi bridges this gap through four key mathematical and conceptual steps: [2, 3, 4] 
## 1. The Ontological Shift (Information is Reality)
In classical physics, information geometry (like the Fisher-Rao metric) is epistemic—it is just used to calculate the "distance" or distinguishability between probability distributions. Obidi rejects this view. He posits that the entropic field $S(x)$ is a real, continuous physical field. The statistical manifold it lives on is the fundamental fabric of the universe. [1, 2, 5, 6] 
## 2. Distinguishability as Physical Distance
In standard information geometry, metrics measure how distinct two quantum or classical states are from each other. Obidi identifies this informational distinguishability as the literal precursor to physical space: [1, 4, 6] 

* **Fisher-Rao Metric (classical information)** and **Fubini-Study Metric (quantum state overlap)** are elevated to represent the actual metric-affine structure of reality. [1, 3] 
* What we perceive as "meters" or "distance" in physical space is actually a measurement of how different or distinguishable two informational states are within the entropic manifold. [6, 7] 

## 3. The $\alpha = 0$ Connection Alignment
Information geometry relies on the **Amari–Čencov $\alpha$-connections**, a family of geometric connections that dictate how probability models change. [2, 6] 

* Obidi mathematically demonstrates that when $\alpha = 0$, the information-geometric connection becomes uniquely torsion-free and metric-compatible.
* This perfectly matches the Levi-Civita connection used in Einstein's General Relativity. By setting $\alpha = 0$, the mathematical laws governing information flow align directly with the laws governing physical spacetime geometry. [2, 6] 

## 4. Coarse-Graining via the Obidi Action
Physical 4D spacetime does not exist at the fundamental level; it emerges dynamically. [2, 8, 9, 10] 

* ToE introduces the Obidi Action, an informational variational principle. **The Obidi Action gives dynamics and physics and physicality to information geometry.** [11, 12] 
* When this high-dimensional informational metric ($g_{IJ}^{\text{entropic}}$) undergoes macro-scale coarse-graining or statistical projection, it condenses into the standard 4D spacetime metric ($g_{\mu\nu}$) that we experience. [2] 
* In this view, gravity is not a physical pulling force; it is an entropic gradient. As the entropic field reconfigures, its informational curvature mathematically yields the physical Einstein-Hilbert action of general relativity. [7, 13, 14] 

Ultimately, for Obidi, "entropy curves existence itself." The universe is a self-updating "statistical" fabric, and what we perceive as mass, energy, and empty space are just different density states of entropic information. [3, 7, 15]
