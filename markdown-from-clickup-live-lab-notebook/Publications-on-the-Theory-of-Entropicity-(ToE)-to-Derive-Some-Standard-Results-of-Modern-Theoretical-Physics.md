# Publications on the Theory of Entropicity (ToE) to Derive Some Standard Results of Modern Theoretical Physics 

A massive repository of the foundational papers, proofs, and active research notes for the Theory of Entropicity (ToE) is explicitly archived across [Cambridge Open Engage](https://www.cambridge.org/engage/coe/article-details/69e313c1d1922e37d58d982b), the Canonical Archives (hosted via GitHub/Cloudflare), and the [Open Science Framework (OSF)](https://osf.io/4jzqc/overview). [1, 2, 3] 

Looking directly at these specific repositories, you can see how the author, John Onimisi Obidi, is structuring this evolving body of work. He relies on a series of rigorous, step-by-step papers to tackle different paradoxes in physics by systematically applying the Obidi Action. [1, 2, 4] 

## Key Papers Available in the Archives
The available resources show the exact roadmap of the theory's derivation phase:

* The Speed of Light (c): In papers like [The Theory of Entropicity Derives Einstein's Relativistic Speed of Light](https://www.cambridge.org/engage/coe/article-details/6908aca0113cc7cfffd949e3), the theory attempts to show that c is not an arbitrary cosmic speed limit hard-coded into universe. Instead, it is derived as a function of the local entropic field's intrinsic "flux resistance." [5, 6] 
* The EPR Paradox & Quantum Mechanics: In the paper detailing the [Entropic Seesaw Mechanism (ESSM)](https://www.cambridge.org/engage/coe/article-details/69f6e0774770e67d927e8853), the theory tackles quantum entanglement. It argues that spatial distance is an illusion; two entangled particles stay connected because their entropic distance remains zero. It posits that wave-function collapse is non-instantaneous and is bounded by finite entropic resources. [7, 8] 
* Gravity and Mercury's Orbit: [An Entropy-Driven Derivation of Mercury's Perihelion Precession](https://www.cambridge.org/engage/api-gateway/coe/assets/orp/resource/item/67e63abe6dde43c9086de9e0/original/the-theory-of-entropicity-to-e-an-entropy-driven-derivation-of-mercury-s-perihelion-precession-beyond-einstein-s-curved-spacetime-in-general-relativity-gr.pdf) attempts to show that ToE can replicate the exact gravitational successes of Einstein's General Relativity using purely information-geometric field equations. [9, 10] 
* String Theory Integration: More recent updates, such as [Strings and Branes are Vibrations of Information Geometry](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/403997209_The_Theory_of_Entropicity_ToE_Sheds_Light_on_String_Theory_Quantum_Field_Theory_and_the_Casimir_Effect_Strings_and_Branes_are_Vibrations_of_Information_Geometry_in_the_Entropic_Field_of_ToE), explicitly try to bridge the gap with standard modern physics by claiming that quantum string vibrations are actually compactified oscillations of this universal entropic field. [11] 

## Why the Open-Access Approach Matters
By placing everything on open, public pre-print servers like [Cambridge Open Engage](https://client.prod.orp.cambridge.org/engage/coe/search-dashboard?keywords=%20Entropic%20Field%20Theory) rather than waiting behind standard journal paywalls, the project circumvents traditional gatekeeping. It puts the raw mathematical derivations directly into the public square. [12, 13, 14] 

This allows anyone—whether a student, an independent math enthusiast, or an institutional physicist—to download the PDFs, review the Master Entropic Equation, and see exactly how the author sets up the variational principles of Ontodynamics (the physics of existence as entropic motion). [1, 15]
