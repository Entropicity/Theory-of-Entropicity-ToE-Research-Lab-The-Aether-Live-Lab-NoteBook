# Publications on the Theory of Entropicity (ToE) to Derive Some Standard Results of Modern Theoretical Physics 

## On the Immensity of Obidi's Undertaking 
There is now absolutely no doubt about the the fact that what Obidi is attempting to do in the Theory of Entropicity (ToE) is not at all elementary. Regardless of its eventual acceptance or critique by mainstream physics, the sheer mathematical and conceptual scope of what John Onimisi Obidi is attempting with the Theory of Entropicity (ToE) is highly complex, sophisticated, and deeply non-elementary. [1, 2, 3, 4, 5] 

To understand why this work is so far removed from basic or elementary physics, one only has to look at the advanced mathematical machinery the author synthesizes to build the theory.
## 1. High-Level Mathematical Synthesis
Obidi does not just write down simple algebraic formulas. To replace spacetime with an information-based reality, his papers pull from some of the most rigorous fields of mathematics: [1] 

* Information Geometry: It treats probability distributions as a curved geometric space, utilizing advanced concepts like the Fisher-Rao metric and Amari-Čencov connections. [1, 6] 
* Spectral Operator Algebra: The theory implements functional analysis and infinite-dimensional operators to try and capture quantum mechanical properties within the entropic field. [1, 7] 
* Differential Geometry & Variational Calculus: Building the Obidi Action requires the same complex calculus of variations used in General Relativity (like the Einstein-Hilbert action), but applied to a localized, changing entropy density rather than space curvature. [6, 8] 

## 2. A Massive Conceptual Inversion
In elementary physics, you learn that things are made of matter, matter occupies space, and as matter rearranges, entropy happens. Obidi’s theory requires a complete mental flip—what he calls an "Ontological Inversion". [5, 9, 10] 

But Obidi asks the reader to treat space, time, gravity, and even the speed of light as secondary, emergent phenomena. In this framework, an electron isn't a solid ball of matter; it is a localized "condensation" or tight knot of entropic constraints. Gravity isn't the bending of an empty fabric, but rather dynamic entropic pressure. Processing that requires a deep familiarity with advanced theoretical physics. [7, 11, 12, 13, 14] 

## 3. Non-Linear, Iterative Field Equations
In standard physics textbooks, many basic problems have clean, elegant, closed-form solutions (like E=mc² or F=ma).

As detailed in the theory's literature, the Master Entropic Equation (MEE) [otherwise called the Obidi Field Equations (OFE)] does not behave this way. Because it deals with information and probability, the field equations are inherently non-linear and must be approached through non-explicit, iterative mathematical methods—resembling how complex algorithms update during Bayesian inference. [15] 

Whether the Theory of Entropicity (ToE) ultimately succeeds or fails in its quest to unify physics, it is a dense, high-level structural framework that demands a profound grasp of thermodynamics, quantum mechanics, and differential geometry to navigate. [1, 8, 16, 17, 18]

# The Canonical Archives of the Theory of Entropicity (ToE)

A massive repository of the foundational papers, proofs, and active research notes for the Theory of Entropicity (ToE) is explicitly archived across [Cambridge Open Engage](https://www.cambridge.org/engage/coe/article-details/69e313c1d1922e37d58d982b), the Canonical Archives (hosted via GitHub/Cloudflare), and the [Open Science Framework (OSF)](https://osf.io/4jzqc/overview). [1, 2, 3] 

Looking directly at these specific repositories, the reader can see how the author, **John Onimisi Obidi**, is structuring this evolving body of work. He relies on a series of rigorous, step-by-step papers to **tackle different paradoxes** in physics by systematically applying the **Obidi Action**. [1, 2, 4] 

## Key Papers Available in the Archives
The available resources show the exact roadmap of the theory's derivation phase:

* The Speed of Light (c): In papers like [The Theory of Entropicity Derives Einstein's Relativistic Speed of Light](https://www.cambridge.org/engage/coe/article-details/6908aca0113cc7cfffd949e3), the theory goes to great lengths to demonstrate that the speed of light (c) is not an arbitrary cosmic speed limit hard-coded into universe. Instead, it is derived as a function of the local entropic field's intrinsic "flux resistance." [5, 6] 
* The EPR Paradox & Quantum Mechanics: In the paper detailing the [Entropic Seesaw Mechanism (ESSM)](https://www.cambridge.org/engage/coe/article-details/69f6e0774770e67d927e8853), the theory tackles quantum entanglement. It argues that spatial distance is an illusion; two entangled particles stay connected because their entropic distance remains zero. It posits that wave-function collapse is non-instantaneous and is bounded by finite entropic resources. [7, 8] 
* Gravity and Mercury's Orbit: [An Entropy-Driven Derivation of Mercury's Perihelion Precession](https://www.cambridge.org/engage/api-gateway/coe/assets/orp/resource/item/67e63abe6dde43c9086de9e0/original/the-theory-of-entropicity-to-e-an-entropy-driven-derivation-of-mercury-s-perihelion-precession-beyond-einstein-s-curved-spacetime-in-general-relativity-gr.pdf) attempts to show that ToE can replicate the exact gravitational successes of Einstein's General Relativity using purely information-geometric field equations. [9, 10] 
* String Theory Integration: More recent updates, such as [Strings and Branes are Vibrations of Information Geometry](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/403997209_The_Theory_of_Entropicity_ToE_Sheds_Light_on_String_Theory_Quantum_Field_Theory_and_the_Casimir_Effect_Strings_and_Branes_are_Vibrations_of_Information_Geometry_in_the_Entropic_Field_of_ToE), explicitly try to bridge the gap with standard modern physics by positing that quantum string vibrations are actually compactified oscillations of this universal entropic field. [11] 

## Why the Open-Access Approach Matters
By placing everything on open, public pre-print servers like [Cambridge Open Engage](https://client.prod.orp.cambridge.org/engage/coe/search-dashboard?keywords=%20Entropic%20Field%20Theory) rather than waiting behind standard journal paywalls, the project circumvents traditional gatekeeping. It puts the raw mathematical derivations directly into the public square. [12, 13, 14] 

This allows anyone—whether a student, an independent math enthusiast, or an institutional physicist—to download the PDFs, review the Master Entropic Equation, and see exactly how the author sets up the variational principles of Ontodynamics (the physics of existence as entropic motion and negotiation). [1, 15]
