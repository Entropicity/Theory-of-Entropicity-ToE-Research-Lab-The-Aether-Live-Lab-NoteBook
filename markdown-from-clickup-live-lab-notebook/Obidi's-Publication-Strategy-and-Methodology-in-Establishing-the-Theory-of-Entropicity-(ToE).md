# Obidi's Publication Strategy and Methodology in Establishing the Theory of Entropicity (ToE)

John Onimisi Obidi utilizes a highly diverse, multi-platform publishing strategy to spread the Theory of Entropicity (ToE). Because his framework attempts a massive paradigm shift—overturning 150 years of thermodynamics and rewriting the foundations of physics—he relies on distinct communication channels tailored to different tiers of readers, from elite academics to the general public. [1, 2, 3, 4, 5] 

## Academic and Technical Audiences
To engage theoretical physicists and mathematicians, Obidi prioritizes peer-review tracking, rigorous mathematical formatting, and scholarly permanence: [1] 

* Open Science Framework (OSF) & SSRN: He utilizes repositories like [OSF](https://osf.io/4jzqc/overview) and SSRN to share foundational papers, such as his Living Review Letters Series and Collected Works on the Evolution of ToE. These outline the heavy-lifting math behind the Obidi Action and the Master Entropic Equation (MEE). [6, 7, 8, 9] 
* Academia.edu: Obidi uploads technical manuscripts, such as his papers on quantum gravitational correspondence and reconciling Einstein and Bohr, directly addressing the established physics community. [10, 11] 
* GitHub Pages: For maximum technical transparency, the mathematical architecture—including the Obidi Curvature Invariant (OCI)—is hosted openly via GitHub repositories using reproducible workflows and precise LaTeX documentation. [1, 3, 12] 

## Semi-Technical and Interdisciplinary Audiences
To reach researchers in adjacent fields like philosophy, artificial intelligence, and digital biology, Obidi translates equations into conceptual frameworks: [11, 13] 

* Substack: Through platforms like his [Substack publication](https://johnobidi.substack.com/p/on-the-mathematical-foundations-of), he breaks down the philosophical motivations of "ontodynamics" (the study of existence as entropic motion). [6, 14] 
* Encyclopedia.pub: He leverages scholarly encyclopedias to feature comparative analyses, mapping how ToE stacks up against or complements other famous modern frameworks, like Donald Hoffman’s Conscious Realism. [15, 16] 

## General and Non-Technical Audiences
To establish broad domain visibility and make a highly complex topic accessible to curious non-physicists, he leans into digital media: [1, 17] 

* Medium: Obidi maintains an active [Medium profile](https://medium.com/@jonimisiobidi) where he publishes long-form essays. He relies on highly punchy, dramatic analogies—such as comparing his methodology to a "Blitzkrieg" over traditional physics—to explain how gravity behaves as "entropic pressure" without losing the reader in complex calculus. [1, 2, 18]
